Professional Biography

Chatri Khatfan

Chatri Khatfan brings several decades of leadership and technical management experience, with a proven track record of driving growth and innovation across multiple companies in Thailand and Japan. He spent three years managing Thai Audiotex Limited and TAS Mobile Limited, rising to the position of top manager. Following a role as project manager at Locus Telecommunications Inc., Chatri was invited back to Thai Audiotex Limited to serve as the top technical manager for both companies, a position he held for nine years.

Since 2010, Chatri has been a co-founder and technical director of Utel Corporation (Japan), where he has played a key role in driving the company’s technical vision and product development.

He earned his Bachelor of Engineering (Electrical and Computer Engineering) degree at Queensland University of Technology (QUT), followed by postgraduate studies in Computer System Engineering at the University of Queensland (UQ). Both institutions are globally recognized for their excellence in engineering and technology education. Supported by a prestigious scholarship that acknowledged his academic excellence, Chatri’s international education provided him with a global perspective and a strong foundation in advanced technical principles. He is fluent in English, which complements his technical expertise and facilitates effective communication in international environments.

Chatri possesses a comprehensive skill set in computer engineering, including expertise in embedded systems design, network architecture, software development, system integration, and hardware troubleshooting. He is proficient in programming languages such as C/C++, Python, and Java, and experienced in IoT solutions, cloud computing platforms, and AI-driven technologies.
