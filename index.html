About

Chatri Khatfan is an accomplished engineer specializing in Electrical & Computer Systems Engineering. Originating from a small province in northern Thailand, his exceptional academic performance earned him a prestigious scholarship to study in Australia. He completed his undergraduate degree at Queensland University of Technology and later pursued postgraduate studies at the University of Queensland. Returning to Thailand in 1999, he quickly established himself as a respected figure in the engineering field. His expertise and leadership qualities were swiftly acknowledged, leading to his appointment as the top manager of two companies within just three years—a testament to his strategic vision and professional excellence.
